Hi! I am a Master's student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Instituto Superior Técnico (IST), University of Lisbon, where I carry out research at the Institute for Systems and Robotics (ISR-Lisbon).

My work focuses on humanoid locomotion, where I train and deploy whole-body control policies using reinforcement learning and optimal control. I am also interested in foundation models for robotics, including LLM-based task planning for general-purpose domestic robots, and I am part of the SocRob@Home team competing in RoboCup@Home.

Previously, I spent a semester as an exchange student at KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, studying systems, control, and robotics.
